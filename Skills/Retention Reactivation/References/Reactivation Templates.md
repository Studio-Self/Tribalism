### Tier 3: Former Power User
```
Hey [name], I miss seeing you around [community]!

You were one of our most [active/helpful/insightful] members, and
your absence is felt.

I wanted to ask directly: is there something we could do to bring
you back? Whether it's:
- Something that changed that didn't work for you
- A format or program you'd like to see
- Life just got busy (totally valid)

Your perspective meant a lot to the community, and it would mean a
lot to me to understand what happened.

Either way, thanks for everything you contributed.
```

## Reactivation Benchmarks

| Tactic | Target Reactivation Rate | Time to Reactivate |
|--------|-------------------------|-------------------|
| Personal DM (recently lapsed) | 15-25% | 1-7 days |
| Personal DM (long-term inactive) | 5-12% | 7-14 days |
| Personal DM (former power user) | 20-35% | 3-14 days |
| Email sequence (3 emails) | 3-8% | 14-30 days |
| Tagged in relevant discussion | 10-20% | 1-3 days |
| Event invitation (relevant topic) | 8-15% | Depends on event date |
| "Comeback" challenge or thread | 5-10% | 7-14 days |
