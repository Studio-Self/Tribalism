# Growth Playbooks

Channel-by-channel playbooks with specific copy, tactics, and benchmarks.

---

## Playbook 1: Member Referral Program

### Setup
1. Create a unique referral link for each member (most platforms support this)
2. Decide on incentive: badge, role upgrade, swag, credit, or recognition
3. Set up tracking (who invited whom)

### Referral Request Templates

**In-Community Prompt (Monthly)**
```
Know someone who'd be a great fit for [community name]?

We grow best through people like you inviting people like them.

Your referral link: [link]

When your invite joins and introduces themselves, you'll get [reward].
```

**Post-Event Referral Ask**
```
Loved tonight's [event]? Know someone who'd get a lot out of conversations
like this?

Share your invite: [link]

No spamming â€” just one or two people who'd genuinely benefit.
```

**Milestone Referral Prompt**
```
Hey [name], you've been a member for [X] months and contributed [X] posts.
You're one of our most active members!

If [community name] has been valuable to you, would you consider inviting
one person who'd be a great fit?

Your personal invite: [link]

Thanks for being such a big part of this community.
```

### Benchmarks
- Referral program awareness: 60-80% of active members should know it exists
- Referral rate: 3-8% of active members will refer someone
- Referral conversion: 40-60% of referred people will join (much higher than cold)
- Referred member retention: 25-35% higher than non-referred members
- Referred member activation: 2x more likely to post in first week

**Post-Event Growth Sequence:**
1. Thank attendees via email (within 2 hours)
2. Share recording + key takeaways
3. CTA: "Enjoyed this? We do these [weekly/monthly]. Join [community] to never miss one."
4. Follow up non-joiners after 7 days with next event announcement

- Registration to attendance rate (virtual): 35-50%
- Event attendee to community join rate: 10-25%
- Attendee activation rate (if they join): 50-70% (much higher than cold joins)
- Cost per community member via events: $5-20 (if free events) vs. $20-80 (paid ads)

## Playbook 5: SEO Growth (Public Communities)

### Optimizing Public Community Content

Applies to: Discourse, Bettermode, Forem, public Circle spaces

**Title optimization:**
- Include the question or problem in the title
- Match how people search: "How to [do X] with [tool/approach]"
- Avoid community-specific jargon in titles

**Content quality:**
- Solved/answered threads rank higher â€” mark solutions
- Comprehensive first posts rank better than short questions
- Internal links between related threads boost SEO
- Fresh content (recent replies) keeps threads ranking

**Category structure for SEO:**
```
/getting-started      â†’ Targets "how to use [product]" queries
/best-practices       â†’ Targets "[topic] best practices" queries
/troubleshooting      â†’ Targets "[product] [error/problem]" queries
/integrations         â†’ Targets "[product] + [tool] integration" queries
/templates-examples   â†’ Targets "[product] template" queries
```

### SEO Growth Benchmarks
- Time to meaningful organic traffic: 6-12 months
- Public community pages indexed: target 80%+ in Google
- Community content vs. blog organic traffic: 40-60% of total for mature communities
- Conversion rate from organic community visitor to member: 2-5%
