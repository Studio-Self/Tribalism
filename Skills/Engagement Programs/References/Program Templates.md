## Discussion Prompts (52 Weeks of Topics)

### Opinion & Debate Prompts
1. What's the most overhyped [trend/tool/practice] in [your field] right now?
2. Unpopular opinion time: What's a best practice you think is actually bad advice?
3. If you had to start over in [field] today, what would you do differently?
4. What's a tool/process everyone loves that you think is overrated?
5. What hill will you die on about [topic]?
6. What's the worst advice you've received in [field]?
7. What conventional wisdom in [industry] is actually wrong?
8. If you could only use 3 tools for [work], what would they be and why?
9. What's something most people in [field] are wrong about?
10. What trend do you think will be dead in 2 years?
11. What's the biggest mistake beginners make in [field]?
12. What's a "rule" in [field] that you regularly break?
13. What's overengineered in your workflow? What's underengineered?

### Experience & Story Prompts
14. What's the biggest win you've had this year? (Big or small)
15. What's a project that completely failed, and what did you learn?
16. Tell us about a time you changed your mind about something important in [field]
17. What's the most useful thing you learned in the last 30 days?
18. What's a small habit that made a big difference in your work?
19. What's the best money you've ever spent on [work/career/craft]?
20. What's something you've built/done that you're secretly proud of?
21. What was your "aha moment" in [field]?
22. What's the hardest feedback you've received, and was it right?
23. What's a lesson you learned the hard way that you wish someone told you earlier?

### Tactical & How-To Prompts
24. Share your [morning routine / workflow / setup / process] in 5 bullet points
25. What's your secret weapon tool/resource that most people don't know about?
26. How do you handle [common challenge in your field]?
27. What's your approach to [specific skill]? Walk us through it.
28. Share a template, checklist, or framework you use regularly
29. What's your tech stack? (List every tool you use daily)
30. How do you stay current in [field]? What do you read/follow/listen to?
31. What's one thing you automated that saved you hours?
32. Show us your [workspace/desktop/dashboard/setup]
33. What's a shortcut or hack you discovered recently?

### Networking & Connection Prompts
34. What are you currently working on? (Share and get feedback)
35. What's one thing you need help with right now?
36. Who's hiring? Who's looking? (Monthly job thread)
37. What would your dream collaboration look like?
38. Introduce yourself to someone new today â€” find 1 thing you have in common
39. What's something you could teach the rest of us?

### Reflective & Strategic Prompts
40. What are your goals for this quarter? Let's hold each other accountable
41. What would you do differently if you were starting [project/career] today?
42. What are you optimistic about in [industry]?
43. What are you worried about in [industry]?
44. What's the one thing that would 10x your [work/career/business]?
45. If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing about [industry], what would it be?
46. What's the one metric/KPI you obsess over?

### Fun & Social Prompts
47. What's on your desk right now? (Photo thread)
48. What's the last book/article/podcast that changed how you think?
49. What did you do this weekend? (Non-work edition)
50. Share a meme that perfectly describes [field/industry]
51. What are you binge-watching/reading right now?
52. Share something you created outside of work

## AMA Templates

### Pre-Event Announcement
```
# AMA: [Guest Name] â€” [Date] at [Time] [Timezone]

We're thrilled to host [guest name] for an AMA!

**About [guest]:**
[2-3 sentence bio. What they've done. Why they're interesting to this community.]

**Topic:** [What they'll be answering questions about]

**How it works:**
- Drop your questions below starting now
- [Guest] will answer live on [date] from [time] to [time]
- Upvote questions you want answered (they'll prioritize top-voted)
- Ask follow-ups in replies

**Rules:**
- One question per message (so people can upvote individual questions)
- Keep questions specific and actionable
- Be respectful of [guest]'s time
```

## Program Engagement Benchmarks

| Program Type | Healthy Participation Rate | Great Participation Rate |
|-------------|--------------------------|------------------------|
| Weekly discussion thread | 5-10% of active members post | >15% |
| Show and Tell | 3-5% share, 15-20% react/comment | >8% share |
| 7-day challenge | 20-30% of signups complete | >50% |
| 30-day challenge | 10-20% of signups complete | >30% |
| AMA | 5-10% ask questions, 20-30% read | >15% ask |
| Weekly goals/check-in | 3-8% post goals, 5-10% post results | >10% |
| Member spotlight | 80%+ positive reactions | >20 comments |
| Coffee chats/pairings | 40-60% of pairs actually meet | >70% |
