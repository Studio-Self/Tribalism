# Community Benchmark Data

Industry benchmarks for community metrics. Use these to evaluate your community's health and set targets.

---

## Engagement Benchmarks

### DAU/MAU Ratio (Daily Active / Monthly Active)

| Community Type | Poor | Average | Good | Great |
|---------------|------|---------|------|-------|
| Product community | <5% | 5-10% | 10-20% | >20% |
| Professional network | <3% | 3-8% | 8-15% | >15% |
| Creator community | <8% | 8-15% | 15-25% | >25% |
| Developer community | <5% | 5-12% | 12-20% | >20% |
| Interest/hobby | <10% | 10-20% | 20-35% | >35% |

**Context:** Slack communities average 8-12% DAU/MAU. Discord communities average 15-25%. Forums average 3-8%.

### Posts Per Active Member Per Month

| Community Type | Low | Average | Healthy | Very Active |
|---------------|-----|---------|---------|-------------|
| Chat-based (Discord/Slack) | <2 | 2-5 | 5-15 | >15 |
| Forum-based (Discourse) | <1 | 1-3 | 3-8 | >8 |
| Platform (Circle/Mighty) | <1 | 1-3 | 3-7 | >7 |

### Reply Rate (Threads That Get At Least One Reply)

| Community Type | Poor | Average | Good | Excellent |
|---------------|------|---------|------|-----------|
| All communities | <30% | 30-50% | 50-70% | >70% |
| Q&A / Support | <40% | 40-60% | 60-80% | >80% |

**Named example:** Salesforce's Trailblazer Community achieves 85%+ reply rate on questions. Stack Overflow averages 75%.

### Content Creation Ratio

| Level | % of Members | Behavior |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Creators | 1-5% | Start discussions, create content, share original work |
| Contributors | 5-15% | Reply, react, share resources, answer questions |
| Consumers | 60-80% | Read, browse, attend events passively |
| Inactive | 15-30% | Joined but not active in last 30 days |

**Named example:** Reddit's creator-to-consumer ratio is roughly 1:100. Niche professional communities average 5-10% creators.

## Growth Benchmarks

### New Member Acquisition Rate

| Stage | Weekly New Members (Healthy) |
|-------|------------------------------|
| Pre-launch (building founding group) | 5-10 (hand-picked) |
| Early (first 3 months) | 10-30 |
| Growing (3-12 months) | 30-100 |
| Scaling (1-3 years) | 100-500 |
| Mature (3+ years) | Varies — focus on quality over quantity |

### Activation Rate (Meaningful Action in First 7 Days)

| Quality | Rate |
|---------|------|
| Poor | <20% |
| Below average | 20-35% |
| Average | 35-50% |
| Good | 50-65% |
| Excellent | >65% |

**Named example:** Figma Community's activation rate (first post within 7 days) is estimated at 40-50%. High-touch paid communities like Reforge average 70-80%.

### Source-Specific Join-to-Active Rates

| Source | Join Rate | Activation Rate | 30-Day Retention |
|--------|-----------|----------------|-----------------|
| Personal invite from member | 40-60% | 50-70% | 45-60% |
| Event attendee | 15-30% | 45-65% | 40-55% |
| In-product prompt | 5-15% | 35-55% | 30-45% |
| Social media | 3-8% | 25-40% | 20-35% |
| Blog/SEO | 2-5% | 20-35% | 18-30% |
| Paid ads | 1-5% | 15-30% | 12-25% |

## Retention Benchmarks

### Retention Curves

| Timeframe | Free Community | Paid Community | Product Community |
|-----------|---------------|---------------|------------------|
| 7-day | 50-70% | 75-90% | 55-75% |
| 30-day | 30-45% | 60-80% | 35-50% |
| 90-day | 20-30% | 45-65% | 25-35% |
| 6-month | 12-20% | 35-50% | 18-25% |
| 12-month | 8-15% | 25-40% | 12-18% |

**Named example:** Lenny Rachitsky's paid community reports ~70% annual retention. Professional Slack communities average 15-25% annual retention. Discord gaming communities average 8-12%.

### Churn Timing

| Drop-off Point | % of Total Churn | Root Cause |
|---------------|-----------------|-----------|
| Day 1 (never activated) | 30-40% | Onboarding failure |
| Week 1-2 (tried, left) | 20-25% | Value not clear fast enough |
| Month 1-3 (faded out) | 15-20% | No habit formed |
| Month 3-12 (lifecycle) | 10-15% | Outgrew, life change, found alternative |
| Month 12+ (veteran churn) | 5-10% | Community stagnation, culture shift |

## Business Impact Benchmarks

### Support Deflection

| Metric | Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|
| % of support questions answered in community | 20-40% (mature communities achieve 50%+) |
| Cost per ticket (traditional support) | $15-25 |
| Cost per community-resolved question | $2-5 |
| Annual savings for 1,000 deflected tickets | $15K-25K |

**Named example:** Atlassian's community answers 60%+ of support questions. MongoDB community deflects ~40% of support volume.

### Retention Lift

| Metric | Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|
| Community members vs. non-members retention | 15-30% higher |
| Community members vs. non-members NPS | 10-25 points higher |
| Community members vs. non-members LTV | 20-40% higher |
| Community members vs. non-members expansion revenue | 15-30% higher |

**Named example:** Gainsight reports community members have 33% lower churn. HubSpot community members have 25% higher NPS.

### Content Value

| Metric | Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|
| Community-generated SEO content value (vs. equivalent paid) | $5-20 per indexed page/month |
| User-generated content production value (vs. equivalent freelancer cost) | $50-500 per quality post |
| Community-sourced case study value | $2K-10K each (vs. agency-produced) |

## Paid Community Benchmarks

### Pricing

| Community Type | Typical Monthly Price | Typical Annual Price |
|---------------|----------------------|---------------------|
| Interest/hobby | $5-15 | $50-120 |
| Professional network | $25-75 | $200-650 |
| Expert community | $50-200 | $400-1,800 |
| Executive/high-end | $200-1,000 | $2,000-10,000 |
| Mastermind/cohort | $500-5,000 | $5,000-50,000 |

### Revenue Benchmarks

| Metric | Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|
| Free → Paid conversion rate | 2-5% (community as product), 10-20% (community as add-on) |
| Monthly churn rate (paid) | 3-8% (healthy), >10% (problematic) |
| Annual churn rate (paid) | 25-50% (healthy), >60% (problematic) |
| LTV:CAC ratio target | >3:1 |
| Revenue per member per month | $20-100 (professional), $5-25 (interest) |

**Named example:** Hampton (Sam Parr's community) charges $8,500/year. Reforge charges $1,995/year. Both report <30% annual churn.
