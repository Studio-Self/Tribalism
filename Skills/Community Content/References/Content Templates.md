## Content Calendar Benchmarks

| Content Type | Optimal Frequency | Engagement Rate (% of active members) |
|-------------|-------------------|--------------------------------------|
| Discussion prompt | 2-3x/week | 5-15% respond, 30-50% read |
| Resource share | 1-2x/week | 3-8% engage (click/comment) |
| Member spotlight | 1x/week or 2x/month | 20-40% view, 5-10% comment |
| Poll | 1x/week | 15-30% vote |
| Weekly newsletter | 1x/week | 40-60% open rate, 8-15% click rate |
| Monthly recap | 1x/month | 50-65% open rate |
| External content (blog/social) | 2-4x/month | Varies by channel |
