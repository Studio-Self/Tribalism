| Time to first post | >72 hours | 24-72 hours | 4-24 hours | <4 hours |
| 7-day activation (took meaningful action) | <20% | 20-40% | 40-60% | >60% |
| 7-day retention (returned after day 1) | <30% | 30-50% | 50-70% | >70% |
| 30-day retention | <15% | 15-30% | 30-45% | >45% |
| Welcome response time | >24 hours | 12-24 hours | 4-12 hours | <4 hours |
| Onboarding email open rate | <30% | 30-45% | 45-60% | >60% |
| Onboarding survey response rate | <10% | 10-20% | 20-35% | >35% |
