# Community Strategy Examples

Filled-out strategy examples for different community types. Use as templates.

---

## Example 1: Product Community (SaaS Tool)

### Strategy Summary
- **Purpose (member):** Get help, learn best practices, connect with peers, influence product roadmap
- **Purpose (business):** Reduce support tickets by 30%, increase retention by 15%, source product feedback
- **Model:** Neighborhood (target: 2K-5K active members)
- **Platform:** Discourse (SEO-friendly, persistent knowledge base)
- **Differentiator:** Only place to get direct product team interaction + peer-sourced workflows

### Member Journey
| Stage | Criteria | Activities | Value Unlocked |
|-------|----------|-----------|---------------|
| Newcomer | Joined, <7 days | Read getting started guide, introduce themselves, ask first question | Access to knowledge base, welcome response within 4 hours |
| Active Member | Posted 5+ times, active 30+ days | Answer questions, share workflows, attend events | Trusted member badge, access to beta features channel |
| Contributor | 50+ posts, helped 10+ people | Write guides, create templates, moderate | Direct Slack access to product team, contributor swag |
| Champion | 6+ months, significant impact | Speak at events, beta test features, mentor newcomers | Advisory board seat, conference sponsorship, paid stipend |

### 90-Day Roadmap
**Month 1:**
- Set up Discourse with 5 categories (Getting Started, Best Practices, Feature Requests, Bug Reports, General)
- Recruit 30 founding members from top customers (personal outreach by CSM team)
- Seed 20 discussions from real support ticket themes
- Launch weekly "Tips & Tricks Tuesday" thread
- Set up Canny integration for feature voting
- Baseline metrics: avg response time, questions answered, satisfaction

**Month 2:**
- Open to all customers (in-app prompt during onboarding)
- Launch monthly AMA with product manager
- Identify and recruit 3-5 active members as beta moderators
- Start weekly digest email (top 5 discussions, upcoming events)
- Track: 40% activation rate target, <4hr response time target

**Month 3:**
- Launch contributor program with swag and beta access
- Start "Community Spotlight" monthly feature
- Publish first "Community Insights" report for product team (top feature requests, common pain points)
- Measure: support ticket deflection, NPS differential for community members vs. non-members
- Goal: 50+ posts/week by community members (not staff)

### Risk Assessment
| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|-----------|
| Low initial engagement | Medium | High | Pre-seed 20 discussions, personally DM founding members daily |
| Product team doesn't respond to feedback | Medium | High | Weekly product team rotation for community Q&A |
| Support team feels threatened | Low | Medium | Position as augmenting support, share deflection data |

## Example 2: Creator Community (Paid Membership)

### Strategy Summary
- **Purpose (member):** Access peer network, get feedback on work, find collaboration opportunities, learn advanced techniques
- **Purpose (business):** $25K MRR from memberships, create content flywheel, build industry authority
- **Model:** Campfire (target: 300-500 active members)
- **Platform:** Circle (native payments, courses, clean UX)
- **Differentiator:** Curated membership (application only), high-signal environment, direct access to industry leaders

### Member Journey
| Stage | Criteria | Activities | Value Unlocked |
|-------|----------|-----------|---------------|
| Applicant | Applied, pending review | Complete application (3 questions), share portfolio | - |
| New Member | Accepted, <14 days | Attend orientation call, introduce themselves, join first study group | All spaces, events, resource library |
| Active Member | 30+ days, 10+ contributions | Participate in critique sessions, share work, attend events | Submit work for community spotlight, access to mentors |
| Inner Circle | 6+ months, exceptional contribution | Lead study groups, host workshops, mentor new members | Annual retreat invitation, speaking opportunities, revenue share |

### 90-Day Roadmap
**Month 1:**
- Build Circle space with 4 spaces: Lobby, Feedback Studio, Resource Library, Events
- Set up application form (3 questions: What do you create? What are you working on? What do you want from this community?)
- Personally recruit 50 founding members at $0 (founding rate, locked forever)
- Host weekly live critique session (Friday 12pm EST)
- Launch #daily-practice channel (accountability)

**Month 2:**
- Open paid applications ($79/month, founding rate $49/month)
- Launch first 4-week cohort program ("Shipped in 30 Days" challenge)
- Start monthly guest masterclass with industry leader
- Goal: 75 paid members by end of month
- Track: application-to-acceptance rate (target 40%), activation rate (target 70%)

**Month 3:**
- Launch mentorship matching (monthly 1:1 pairings)
- Create resource library from member-contributed templates and guides
- First community showcase event (members present finished work publicly)
- Publish "Community Insights" externally (drives growth through content loop)
- Goal: 120 paid members, $6K MRR

### Pricing
- Monthly: $79/month
- Annual: $690/year (save $258, ~27% discount)
- Founding: $49/month locked forever (first 50 members)
- Student: $39/month (verified)

## Example 3: Professional Network (B2B)

### Strategy Summary
- **Purpose (member):** Build professional network, stay current on industry trends, find mentors and collaborators, access job opportunities
- **Purpose (business):** Lead generation for consulting services, industry authority, event sponsorship revenue
- **Model:** Neighborhood (target: 1K-3K active members)
- **Platform:** Slack (familiar to professionals, low friction, Slack Connect for partnerships)
- **Differentiator:** Practitioner-only (no vendors, no recruiters unless invited), signal-to-noise ratio

### Member Journey
| Stage | Criteria | Activities | Value Unlocked |
|-------|----------|-----------|---------------|
| Newcomer | Joined via invite/application, <7 days | Introduce themselves, browse #hot-takes, join 2 topic channels | General channels, resource library, event access |
| Member | Active 30+ days, 5+ contributions | Participate in weekly discussions, attend events | Access to #jobs channel, mentorship matching |
| Contributor | 3+ months, known by name | Share expertise, host AMAs, contribute resources | Speaker opportunities, advisory board nomination |
| Leader | 6+ months, elected or appointed | Moderate channels, run programs, represent community | Speaking fees from events, leadership retreat, consulting referrals |

### Channel Structure
```
WELCOME
├── #welcome (orientation, read-only)
├── #introductions (new member intros)
├── #announcements (team updates)

DISCUSSIONS
├── #general (main discussion)
├── #industry-trends (news and analysis)
├── #hot-takes (opinions and debates)
├── #career-advice (mentorship, transitions)
├── #resources (tools, articles, templates)

NETWORKING
├── #jobs (vetted opportunities only)
├── #collaboration (find project partners)
├── #events (industry events and meetups)

META
├── #feedback (community suggestions)
├── #random (off-topic, social)
```

## Strategy Comparison Matrix

| Dimension | Product Community | Creator Community | Professional Network |
|-----------|------------------|------------------|---------------------|
| Model | Neighborhood | Campfire | Neighborhood |
| Platform | Discourse | Circle | Slack |
| Size target | 2K-5K | 300-500 | 1K-3K |
| Revenue model | Free (funded by product) | Paid membership ($79/mo) | Free + event sponsorship |
| Staffing | 1 CM + product team rotation | 1 CM + guest hosts | 1 CM + volunteer moderators |
| Monthly budget | $500 (platform + tools) | $200 (platform) | $100 (tools only, free Slack) |
| Key metric | Support tickets deflected | MRR + member satisfaction | Active members + referral rate |
| Growth channel | In-product prompt | Content + referral | Invite-only + event-led |
| Time to self-sustaining | 6-9 months | 3-6 months | 9-12 months |
